# Planning Training Sessions.


1\. **MyCoach Pro** gives you the ability to fully manage your squad's calendar, in particular by using the event scheduling feature.


2\. Click on the **Calendar** module.

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2025-05-13/132f5eea-682f-436d-aebc-1f46d2e7e689/user_cropped_screenshot_55669cba0e5e4df1a9731b392610129c_text_export.jpeg)


3\. To start scheduling events, two options are available:

1. Click on a date and/or time slot and select ***Schedule sessions***
2. Click the **+** button and select ***Schedule sessions***

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2025-05-13/3aebdc8f-5afe-43ea-8376-ad3fcbe5063a/user_cropped_screenshot_dd642eddce0443e094c8a2b05206004e_text_export.jpeg)


#### Simple Recurrence


4\. Start configuring your schedule by selecting:

1. **The team concerned**
2. **The call-up type**
3. **The attendance request**
4. **The dates**

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2025-05-12/0bf7c9cb-047c-4b91-a406-58762edef31f/user_cropped_screenshot_5c3113d1cd2b433dbb660c06940ee4fd_text_export.jpeg)


5\. Leave the **Recurrence** option set to *Every week on selected days.*

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2025-05-12/f339f640-52d2-44ca-819e-12deb02b2f63/user_cropped_screenshot_20e9ee3161ee4ee88526db1934a5713b_text_export.jpeg)


6\. Then configure:

- The days for which you want to schedule your sessions
- The session itself (type, theme, tags, time and duration, form, facilities)
- Player visibility

Then click **NEXT** to see a summary of your schedule, or **DONE** to confirm it.

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2025-05-12/17bc5f2a-dd94-440d-afb2-a20f09dc2b05/screenshot_e19522ff5174417d92dfae80566e0bfb_text_export.jpeg)


#### Advanced Recurrence


7\. Advanced recurrence allows you to schedule multiple different sessions per day.\
Including differentiation between tags, session types, or forms...


8\. At the **Recurrence** selection step, choose the option *Multiple different recurring events.*\
\
Then click **NEXT**.

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2025-05-12/79ff2dd9-ee4d-4b27-9e30-91ad5d93411d/user_cropped_screenshot_10f8dc1652e8486d8458911cbe04b359_text_export.jpeg)


9\. In the same way as for simple recurrence, select the days for which you want to schedule sessions.\
\
You can then configure them independently of each other.

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2025-05-12/c03977cc-bb40-474c-9c71-57d18c40512f/screenshot_6a9c688eb9f942a6b586a4a8b1fbc9d2_text_export.jpeg)


Tip: If a day repeats, a button lets you copy it and paste it onto another day.


10\. If needed, configure your periods or exclude them from the schedule.

Then click **NEXT** to see a summary of your schedule, or **DONE** to confirm it.

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2025-05-12/ca04e6e7-2659-4321-984c-308d8b8bae58/screenshot_e378b0e1f67a4187bb9c480984058071_text_export.jpeg)
