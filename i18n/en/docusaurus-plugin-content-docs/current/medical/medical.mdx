# How it works

### The medical module lets you:
1. **Create and track a pathology** (injury or illness) for a player  
2. **Schedule consultations** (care / follow-up activities) and link them to the medical context  
3. **Analyze the season** through epidemiology (exposure, incidence, burden)

Confidentiality is central: each profile only accesses the information needed for their role.

## Overall logic

###  [I create a pathology (injury or illness)](/docs/medical/pathologies)
The pathology is the central follow-up record.

###  [Sports availability is managed by the pathology](/docs/medical/pathologies)
When created, the player is marked as unavailable.  
The pathology is closed when the actual return date is entered.

###  [I schedule consultations if needed](/docs/medical/consultations)
Consultations are care / follow-up events.  
They can be linked to a pathology to ensure traceability.

###  [I view epidemiology](/docs/medical/epidemiologie)
The indicators make it possible to measure injury frequency and their impact on the squad.
