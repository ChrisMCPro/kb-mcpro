# Epidemiology & indicators

This section turns the entered data into indicators to support prevention and squad monitoring.

All indicators are normalized to 1000 hours of activity so they can be compared over time and across groups.

## Prerequisite: severity scale

The module relies on a configurable **severity scale** based on the number of days unavailable.  
Common example:

- 1 to 7 days: minor
- 7 to 25 days: moderate
- 25 days or more: severe

## Indicator definitions

### Exposure (in hours)
Total training and match duration.

When multiple sources exist, the following priority is applied:

1. GPS duration
2. Individually entered duration (for example: load / RPE)
3. Planned session duration

### Incidence (frequency)
Measures how often injuries occur.

Formula:
`(Number of injuries / Total exposure) × 1000`

### Burden (impact)
Measures the impact of injuries on the squad by including days unavailable.

Formula:
`(Days unavailable / Total exposure) × 1000`

## Team-level view

The indicators make it possible to track:

- volume (injuries, illnesses, players affected)
- collective exposure
- collective incidence
- collective burden

## Player-level view

The same indicators can also be viewed individually in order to:

- track the player’s season (history)
- compare contexts (overall / training / match) depending on your setup
