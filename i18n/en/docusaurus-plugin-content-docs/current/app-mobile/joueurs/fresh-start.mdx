# Getting started with the mobile app

:::info Who is this for?
This guide is intended for **players** who are logging in to the MyCoach Pro AMS app for the first time.
:::

---

## Step 1 — Activate your account

You will receive a welcome email containing:

- **Your username** (it may differ from your email address)
- **A link to create your password** (valid for 24 hours)

Click the link to open the password creation page.

![Welcome email MyCoach Pro](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2024-07-18/792cf9e5-7392-491d-b5fb-0c19be3a3fd4/user_cropped_screenshot_2f851d8ec05d4914ac233aa74ef1df65_text_export.jpeg)

---

## Step 2 — Create your password

On the page that opens:

1. Enter your password (follow the required criteria).
2. Confirm your password.
3. Click **SEND**.

![Password creation page](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2024-07-18/73ff59bf-2c75-45ef-bf65-977e67c4ca01/user_cropped_screenshot_91f1005406d4478eb9e31ce952213719_text_export.jpeg)

---

## Step 3 — Confirmation

Your password is created. You can now download the app.

![Account creation confirmation](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2024-08-09/1b22b161-d05c-40f1-b3c0-75e7b094e6af/user_cropped_screenshot_d32d1c2040c740c4ab29cf171df5c167_text_export.jpeg)

---

## Step 4 — Download the app

The **MyCoach Pro AMS** app is available on:

- 📱 [App Store (iOS)](https://apps.apple.com/fr/app/mycoach-pro-ams/id1643524841)
- 🤖 [Google Play (Android)](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.mycoachpro.ams)

![iOS and Android stores](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2024-07-18/eeddbbc5-72a0-4e53-95d3-61dd96c82183/user_cropped_screenshot_849c4fc6698047a794230ef4531a4ed5_text_export.jpeg)

---

## Step 5 — Sign in

Once the app is installed:

1. Open **MyCoach Pro AMS**.
2. Enter your credentials (username + password).
3. Tap **Sign in**.

![Login screen](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2024-07-18/3ba3d4e5-47e9-4b3c-8789-1023804e5a54/mobile_screenshot_ee41fb87de4940c1aaf857322fa196cf_text_export.jpeg)

---

## Step 6 — Allow notifications

To avoid missing any invitation or form request, tap **Allow** when the app asks.

![Notification permission request](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2024-07-18/6353c225-1abb-4849-a318-16844c02cbfc/mobile_screenshot_742ac32f890b4b38be480d805e949c51_text_export.jpeg)

:::tip Login issue?
Make sure your **username** is correct — it may differ from your email address. If in doubt, contact your staff.
:::
