# Polar & PlayerData setup for FIFA referees.


Two short setups. One time only. 
From this guide you'll connect your devices to MyCoach Pro, filter your sessions, and get the most out of your training data.


---


## Before you start


> ⚠️ **REQUIRED FIRST STEP - Update your MyCoach Pro app**
>
> Version **1.134.0** or later - required to access Polar and PlayerData integrations.
>
> **How to check:** open the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android), search *MyCoach Pro AMS*, tap **Update** if available.

# 1 · Polar


**Connect. Configure. Use.**


Heart rate, pace, route map, HR zones - all flowing directly from Polar Flow into MyCoach Pro.


## The flow in six steps


1. Open Integrations
2. Tap Polar
3. Sign in to Polar
4. Accept cookies
5. Authorize access
6. Linked ✓


---


## 1.1 · Connect to Polar


### Step 1 - Open Integrations


From your Dashboard, tap the sync icon next to **Synchronized Sessions**.


<img src={polar_connect_01_open_integrations} alt="Dashboard · MyCoach Pro" width="315" />

*Dashboard · MyCoach Pro*


---


### Step 2 - Select Polar


Tap **Polar** in your Integrations list.


<img src={polar_connect_02_select_polar} alt="Integrations · MyCoach Pro" width="315" />

*Integrations · MyCoach Pro*


---


### Step 3 - Sign in to Polar


> ⚠️ **IMPORTANT - Use your own Polar Flow account**
>
> Email and password from **Polar Flow** - not your MyCoach Pro login. Tap **Connexion**.


<img src={polar_connect_03_sign_in} alt="Polar Flow authentication" width="315" />

*Polar Flow authentication*


---


### Step 4 - Accept cookies


Polar Flow asks for cookie consent before continuing. **Allow selection** is enough - necessary cookies only.


<img src={polar_connect_04_cookies} alt="Polar Flow cookie consent" width="315" />

*Polar Flow cookie consent*


---


### Step 5 - Authorize MyCoach Pro


> ⚠️ **IMPORTANT - Accept the Terms and Conditions**
>
> Check **I agree to the Terms and Conditions** then tap **OK**. This authorizes Polar to share your training data with MyCoach Pro.


<img src={polar_connect_05_authorize} alt="Polar authorization screen" width="315" />

*Polar authorization screen*


---


### Step 6 - You're linked


Polar now shows as **Linked** in your Integrations. Your future sessions will sync automatically from Polar Flow.


<img src={polar_connect_06_linked} alt="Polar = Linked" width="315" />

*Polar = Linked*


---


## 1.2 · Filter your data sources


To avoid duplicate sessions, configure your dashboard to only show Polar sessions.


### Step 1 - Open Filters


From your Dashboard, tap the **gear icon** next to Synchronized Sessions - not the sync icon next to it.


<img src={polar_filter_01_open_filters} alt="Dashboard · gear icon" width="315" />

*Dashboard · gear icon*


---


### Step 2 - Open Integrations dropdown


In the Filters panel, tap the **Integrations** dropdown to choose which source to display.


<img src={polar_filter_02_open_integrations} alt="Filters panel" width="315" />

*Filters panel*


---


### Step 3 - Select Polar only


Pick **Polar** - leave **Apple Health** unselected to avoid duplicates. Tap **Done**.


<img src={polar_filter_03_select_polar} alt="Integrations dropdown" width="315" />

*Integrations dropdown*


---


### Step 4 - Validate


Tap **Validate**. Your Synchronized Sessions list now shows only Polar sessions.


<img src={polar_filter_04_validate} alt="Filtered dashboard" width="315" />

*Filtered dashboard*


---


> ⚠️ **HEADS UP - What you'll see in your list**
>
> Only sessions uploaded to Polar Flow **after** you connected your account to MyCoach Pro will appear. Limited to the **last 30 days**.
>
> Older sessions, or sessions from before your connection, won't be retrieved.


---


## 1.3 · Your post-session routine


Five steps. Familiar flow - same as today, with richer data.


| # | Step | What you do |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Run** | Run your Polar session as usual. |
| 2 | **Sync** | Sync your watch with Polar Flow. |
| 3 | **Open** | Tap the notification, or find it in MyCoach Pro. |
| 4 | **Review** | See the rich data: HR zones, pace, map. |
| 5 | **Create** | Tap **Create player session**. Done. |


---


### Steps 1 & 2 - Run, then sync your watch


After your session, sync your Polar watch with Polar Flow as you always do. **No change here.**


<img src={polar_routine_01_sync_watch} alt="Polar Flow · Synced 4 min ago" width="315" />

*Polar Flow · Synced 4 min ago*


---


### Step 3 - Open your session


Two ways to get there - pick whichever fits your moment.


**⚡ Fastest: tap the MyCoach Pro notification**


The moment your watch finishes syncing with Polar Flow, MyCoach Pro detects the new session and pushes a notification: *"Your session is ready to be imported. Tap to synchronize it."* Tap it - you land directly on the session detail.


<img src={polar_routine_02b_notification} alt="MyCoach Pro notification on top of Polar Flow" width="315" />

*MyCoach Pro notification on top of Polar Flow*


**Or: open MyCoach Pro and find your session**


If you missed the notification, no problem. Open MyCoach Pro - your Polar session appears in **Synchronized Sessions**, marked **Not imported** until you open it.


<img src={polar_routine_02_find_session} alt="MyCoach Pro dashboard" width="315" />

*MyCoach Pro dashboard*


---


### Step 4 - See the richer session data


Total distance, pace, cadence, speed, altitude - far beyond what you had before. Sourced directly from Polar.


<img src={polar_routine_03_session_detail_kpis} alt="Session detail · core KPIs" width="315" />

*Session detail · core KPIs*


Scroll the session detail for full HR zone breakdown, pace evolution, and the GPS route on map.


<img src={polar_routine_04_session_detail_hr_map} alt="Session detail · HR zones & map" width="315" />

*Session detail · HR zones & map*


---


### Step 5 - Create your player session


Tap **Create player session**, fill in the form the same way as today, then **Done**. Nothing changes here.


<img src={routine_create_player_session} alt="Create player session form" width="315" />

*Create player session form*

# 2 · PlayerData


**Connect. Configure. Use.**


GPS metrics - distance, sprints, accelerations, plus heart rate when paired with a chest strap. All new data in MyCoach Pro.


## The flow in four steps


1. Open Integrations
2. Tap Player Data
3. Sign in
4. Linked ✓


---


## 2.1 · Connect to PlayerData


### Step 1 - Open Integrations


From your Dashboard, tap the sync icon next to **Synchronized Sessions** - same entry point as Polar.


<img src={playerdata_connect_01_open_integrations} alt="Dashboard · MyCoach Pro" width="315" />

*Dashboard · MyCoach Pro*


---


### Step 2 - Select Player Data


Tap **Player Data** in your Integrations list.


<img src={playerdata_connect_02_select_playerdata} alt="Integrations · MyCoach Pro" width="315" />

*Integrations · MyCoach Pro*


---


### Step 3 - Sign in to PlayerData


> ⚠️ **IMPORTANT - Use your PlayerData credentials**
>
> Use the same email and password you use in the **PlayerData app**. Tap **Sign in**.


<img src={playerdata_connect_03_sign_in} alt="PlayerData authentication" width="315" />

*PlayerData authentication*


---


### Step 4 - You're linked


**Player Data** now shows as **Linked** in your Integrations.


<img src={playerdata_connect_04_linked} alt="Player Data = Linked" width="315" />

Same flow as Polar - three taps to keep only PlayerData sessions in your dashboard.


| # | Step | What you do |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Open Filters** | Tap the gear icon next to Synchronized Sessions. |
| 2 | **Open Integrations** | Tap the Integrations dropdown in the Filters panel. |
| 3 | **Select Player Data** | Pick Player Data only. Tap **Done**, then **Validate**. |


---


## 2.3 · Your post-session routine


Same five-step flow as Polar - same **Create player session** at the end.


| # | Step | What you do |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Run** | Run your session with the PlayerData GPS unit. |
| 2 | **Check** | Open the PlayerData app to confirm sync. |
| 3 | **Find** | Open MyCoach Pro, find your session. |
| 4 | **Review** | HR + new GPS metrics: sprints, accel, distance. |
| 5 | **Create** | Tap **Create player session**. Done. |


---


### Steps 1 & 2 - Run, then check your session


Run your session with your PlayerData GPS unit. Open the PlayerData app - your session appears under **Last Session**.


<img src={playerdata_routine_01_app_home} alt="PlayerData app · Last Session" width="315" />

*PlayerData app · Last Session*


---


### Step 3 - Find your session in MyCoach Pro


Your PlayerData session appears in **Synchronized Sessions** - look for the green **PlayerData badge** on the session card.


<img src={playerdata_routine_02_find_session} alt="MyCoach Pro dashboard · PlayerData badge" width="315" />

*MyCoach Pro dashboard · PlayerData badge*


---


### Step 4 - See your heart rate data


> ⚠️ **IMPORTANT - Heart rate via PlayerData has a known limitation**
>
> When paired with a chest strap, PlayerData captures HR alongside GPS - but this connection is currently unreliable on iPhones due to a known Bluetooth issue. HR data may be partial or missing on some sessions.
>
> → If you wear your **Polar watch + HR strap** in parallel during the same session, your full HR data will still come through via your **Polar integration**.


<img src={playerdata_routine_03_heart_rate} alt="Session detail · heart rate" width="315" />

*Session detail · heart rate*

### Step 5 - Discover the new GPS metrics


Acceleration events, decelerations, max speed, sprint distance, high-intensity runs, metres per minute, heart rate zones, total distance - **all new with PlayerData**.


<img src={playerdata_routine_04_gps_metrics} alt="Session detail · GPS metrics" width="315" />

*Session detail · GPS metrics*

## How to insert the images in Coda


Coda doesn't import images automatically from this markdown. You have to add them manually after the import.


**Quick workflow:**


1. **Import this markdown** into a new Coda page (Insert → Import → Paste markdown, or drag the `.md` file)
2. **Unzip** the `coda_screenshots.zip` somewhere on your computer
3. **For each `[📸 IMAGE: filename.png]` line**, do the following:
   - Delete the placeholder line
   - In its place, drag-and-drop the matching file from the unzipped folder
   - Coda will embed the image automatically
4. **Tip:** the filenames are sorted in reading order - easy to follow top to bottom
