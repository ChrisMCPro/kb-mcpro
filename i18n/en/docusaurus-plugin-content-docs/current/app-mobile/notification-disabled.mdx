# My players are no longer receiving notifications

## Identify the issue

### 1. Who is affected? Since when?
The most important first step is to clearly identify **which players in your squad** are actually having a problem receiving MyCoach Pro notifications.

Check:

- Who is affected?
- Since when?
- Does it happen for all events, or only for a specific event type?

### 2. Check that notifications are enabled on the player’s phone
The most likely case is that the user has not enabled notifications, or has refused them on their mobile device.

## How to allow notifications on the device

### iOS (iPhone)

1. Open the **Settings** app.
2. Go to **Apps** (latest iOS version), or find **MyCoach Pro - AMS** directly.
3. Tap **MyCoach Pro - AMS**.
4. Tap **Notifications**.
5. At the top, check **Allow Notifications**.  
   If the button is grey, tap it to enable notifications.

### Android

> The exact path may vary depending on the device model. The steps below describe the most common setup.

1. Find the **MyCoach Pro - AMS** app.
2. Press and hold the app to open the context menu.
3. Tap **App info**.
4. Open the **Notifications** section.
5. Find **All notifications from MyCoach Pro - AMS** and enable it if needed.

If you have just enabled notifications:

- Fully **close the app**.
- Reopen **MyCoach Pro - AMS**.
- The app should ask again for notification permission.
- Tap **Allow**.

## If it still does not work

If notifications are already enabled on the device and the issue is still there, try this last step in the mobile app:

1. Open **MyCoach Pro - AMS**.
2. Tap **Menu**.
3. Open **Diagnostic**.
4. Tap **Resynchronize notification token**.

## If support is needed

If the issue is still not solved, contact [support@mycoachpro.io](mailto:support@mycoachpro.io) and share as much detail as possible:

- Since when the problem started
- Which users are affected and which device they use
- Which events are concerned (type, date, time)

This helps the support team act faster and more efficiently.
