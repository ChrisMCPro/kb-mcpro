# How to Log In?

MyCoach Pro platform is available on PC and tablet.

The MyCoach Pro mobile apps are available on iOS and Android stores.

### [Home](category/accueil)

- Customizable widgets per user
- Alerts based on questionnaire responses
- Shortcuts to all main features

---

### [Calendar](category/calendrier)

- Visualization of all events (training, match, extra-sport, tracking, schooling)
- Individual player schedule with availability
- Automatic notifications
- Infrastructure view

---

### [Exercises](category/exercises)

- Easy creation of exercise sheets (images, videos, PDF)
- Built-in visual editor
- Custom tags for filtering
- Automatic sharing with players

---

### [Medical Module](category/medical)

- Editable medical status: fit / injured / re-athletization
- Integration of consultations, injury history
- Anthropometric data tracking
- Customizable RTT / RTP planning
- Individual and collective statistics
- Document sharing: MRI, scanner, videos
- Communication space between medical and sports staff

---

### [Forms](category/insights)

- Questionnaire creation: Fitness status, RPE, mental, administrative
- Response from mobile or web (player, staff, locker room tablet)
- Custom alerts displayed in dashboard
- Data visualizable on events, sessions, matches

---

### [BI Report](category/insights)

- Aggregation of questionnaire responses
- Free dashboard construction
- External data import
- Comparison, visualization, custom analysis

---

### Messaging

- Secure staff ↔ players discussion
- Contact book and auto-created groups
- New message notifications

---

### [Media Center](category/média-center)

- Video, PDF, image storage
- Collective or individual content assignment
- Accessible on mobile (players) and web (staff)

---

### Administrative

- Player and staff profiles
- Accommodation and schooling tracking
- School timetable
